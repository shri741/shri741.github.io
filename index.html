About ProductFlow

Productflow AI is aware of the difficulties that product managers deal with daily. You're inundated with feature requests, user reviews, and market research all the time. It can be intimidating to sort through all of this information and decide which features are most important.

To give product managers a data-driven, effective method for prioritizing features and creating successful products, we created Productflow AI. Our goal is to make feature prioritization for product managers easier and more efficient
